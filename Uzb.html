Uzbekistan tragedy:

On December 28,2022 the UZBEKISTAN health ministry claimed that there had been a death of 18 children in their country after consuming a cough syrup manufactured and supplied from INDIA named Doc-1 Max. It is said that 18 out of 21 children who had been consuming this cough syrup for treatment of acute respiratory disease had died. The syrup was found to be manufactured by Noida based Marion Biotech and was imported by Quramax Medical LLC.

After further investigations, the ministry declared that the deceased children were found to be consuming the syrup at home without a doctor's prescription for 2-7 days, at least 3-4 times a day and at a volume of 2-2.5ml which exceeds the standard dose for children. Also, the investigations reveal that the consumed batch of the syrups contained a highly toxic substance, ETHYLENE GLYCOL.
